Product description

The Kawai CS8 Digital Piano is a feature packed instrument for musicians of all styles. Designed with the home user in mind, the CS8 features USB connectivity to play MP3 or WAV files directly out of the 6 speakers. The Grand Feel II wooden keys have a seesaw motion and weighted feel for true acoustic piano authenticity. The Ivory Touch surface is comfortable and gently absorbs your natural perspiration for increased playing control. The grand feel, soft and sostenuto pedals offer the detailed characteristics of an acoustic grand piano. The CS8's gloss black finish and closed back stand is a smart look.

Product details

Touch

The Grand Feel II keyboard action provides you with a similar feel to an acoustic grand piano. The wooden keys’ bass note counterweights and graded hammer provide a smoother action that’s similar to an acoustic piano. The triple-sensor key detection produces a range of responsive sounds that are receptive to subtle nuances in the way that you play. Extra long keys allow a more sensitive feel, as well as assisting with the Touch Curve feature. Ivory Touch key surfaces are smooth and allow a natural feel for your playing, as well as absorbing fingertip perspiration to assist playing control.

Tone

The Kawai CS8’s 88 keys have professional touch curves for a natural sound. The five touch curve presets allow users to access some of Kawai’s most respected sounds, and produce them with equal amounts of accuracy and quality. The touch curves have been designed to produce the most precise sound of each piano. The Kawai CS8’s sound has been modelled after two of the most respected acoustic pianos around: the Shigeru Kawai SK-EX and Shigeru Kawai SK-5. These Classic Series pianos produce exceptional clarity with precise tone. The CS8 can be as complex or modest as each user prefers, making it suitable for a variety of playing styles and abilities.

LCD Display

The CS8’s digital screen makes editing and navigation easier than ever. The dedicated buttons to control the metronome, recording functions and effects provide easy access to the different features. The screen and controls are in an optimum position for using quickly in a performance or recording situation. The backlighting on the screen makes it easy to read in different lighting.

In-Built Lessons

Developing your skills has never been easier thanks to Kawai’s lesson features. The Concert Magic has 88 integrated songs, varying from classical to Christmas, with adjustable rhythm and tempo. The Concert Magic intuitively adjusts to your playing, making it suitable for anyone wanting to enhance their sense of rhythm. The on board Alfred’s Basic Piano lessons offer a more traditional layout for learning music, whilst still remaining easy to use and accessible to all.
