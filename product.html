Product features

  • Tone generator Pure CF Engine, which, thanks to sampling, makes it possible to obtain dynamic expression of the highest level.
  • The presence of reverb effect and emulator resonance of the damper, due to which the sound becomes deep and spatial.
  • A recording mode that allows you to save songs in your own performance.
  • Built-in intelligent control over acoustics, which provides good sound quality, regardless of the volume level.
